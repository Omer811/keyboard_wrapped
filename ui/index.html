Cube Stories

Each cube is a chapter in your keyboard’s year. Scroll, reorder, and let the AI weave the stats into a narrative of your typing.

Serve with python3 -m http.server and open ui/index.html. Run gpt_insights.py (with OPENAI_API_KEY) to refresh the AI cube; the data folder ships with sample summaries and fallback replies for testing.
